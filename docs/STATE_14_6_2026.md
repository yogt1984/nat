## The binding constraint
**Execution feasibility (adverse selection), itself gated behind data accumulation.** The IC≈0.45
directional signal (`imbalance_qty_l1`, 1–5s, ~30s half-life) is real and stable — 7/8 robustness
checks on BTC, 6/8 on ETH/SOL — but conditional on mid-cross fills it collapses to **~0.03**; taker
execution (~11 bps RT) is economically impossible against a 0.5–2 bps signal move. Resolving it needs,
in order: (a) a **trade-flow fill model** on the raw trades collected since 2026-06-09 (tradeable if
conditional-IC **> 0.15**), and (b) the **7-day clean streak by Jun-17** to revalidate
`hierarchical_combiner` (fast L2 entry gated by slow L1 direction — the first algo structurally built
to beat adverse selection, currently validated on only 2 days). Everything downstream
(combination → paper → live) is blocked on a positive conditional-IC verdict.
