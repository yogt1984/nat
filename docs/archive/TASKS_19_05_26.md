### What the research achieved:
- Closed taker cost gap from 13x (raw imbalance) to 7.5x (trade_through) to 9.9x (BTC→SOL lead-lag)
- Identified trade_through_imbalance as independent information (queue depletion)

### The fundamental constraint:
**IC×σ ≈ 0.8 bps at every horizon.** Signal decays faster than variance grows. This is market efficiency — the cross-symbol information propagation is priced in within ~0.8 bps.

### What would need to change:
- **Fee < 0.8 bps RT**: No venue currently offers this. Binance VIP9 at 1.61 bps is closest (2.3x gap).
- **10x stronger signal**: IC=0.3+ at 120s+ horizon. Current IC=0.035 at 120s. Would need qualitatively different signal (news, funding events, liquidation cascades).
- **Colocation + maker rebate**: Direct exchange connectivity with queue priority and -0.02 bps rebate. Requires infrastructure investment far beyond current setup.
- **Regime-specific strategies**: Edge is ~0.96 bps in low-entropy regimes. If a venue offered 0.9 bps RT fees, this would be marginally profitable ~30% of the time.
