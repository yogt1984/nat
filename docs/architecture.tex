\section{Overview}

NAT is a quantitative research platform for extracting alpha signals from Hyperliquid perpetual futures. The system comprises four layers:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Data Ingestion} --- Rust real-time ingestor computing 217 features at 100ms intervals
    \item \textbf{Signal Discovery} --- Python-based research pipelines, algorithm framework, and autonomous agents
    \item \textbf{Liquidity Engine} --- Alpha pipeline with 9-step quality gates producing validated signals
    \item \textbf{Execution} --- Paper trading, portfolio assembly, and live order placement
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Output Format}

Parquet files organized as \texttt{data/features/YYYY-MM-DD/Symbol\_HH\_MM.parquet}. Row groups of 10,000 rows ($\sim$5.5 min for 3 symbols), zstd compressed, hourly rotation.

\section{Layer 2: Signal Discovery}

\subsection{Convolver}

Pattern-matching algorithm using data-driven SVD decomposition:

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Offline}: Detect 6 event types (breakouts, turtle soup, traps) $\to$ extract $W$=20 candle windows $\to$ SVD on 4 channels (body, upper wick, lower wick, volume) $\to$ IC gate + BH-FDR correction ($q=0.05$) $\to$ 6 surviving kernels
    \item \textbf{Online}: Accumulate ticks $\to$ form 60s candles $\to$ score sliding window against kernels via cosine similarity $\to$ 8 real-time features
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Agent System}

Three autonomous daemons operating at different timeframes:

\begin{longtable}{|l|c|c|l|}
\hline
\textbf{Agent} & \textbf{Horizon} & \textbf{Generators} & \textbf{Gate Protocol} \\
\hline
Microstructure & 5s & 6 & 5-gate (IC, temporal, symbol, orthogonality, cost) \\
Medium-Freq & 1m--1h & 3 & 4-gate (IC, temporal, symbol, cost) \\
Macro & 1h--24h & 3 & 4-gate (IC, temporal, symbol, cost) \\
\hline
\end{longtable}

Meta Agent orchestrates cross-agent budget allocation, correlation dedup, and portfolio assembly.

\subsection{Discovery Orchestrator}

Continuous 6-hour cycles: DATA\_HEALTH $\to$ SIGNAL\_SWEEP $\to$ TRAINING $\to$ BACKTESTING $\to$ ALPHA\_PIPELINE $\to$ REPORTING $\to$ SLEEPING.

\section{Layer 3: Liquidity Engine}

\subsection{Alpha Pipeline (9 Steps)}

\begin{longtable}{|c|l|l|l|}
\hline
\textbf{Step} & \textbf{Name} & \textbf{Method} & \textbf{Gate} \\
\hline
1 & Screening & FDR on feature IC & G1: $\geq$5 significant features \\
2 & Combining & IC-weighted, correlation dedup & G2: IC ratio $\geq 0.8\times$ max \\
3 & Sizing & Kelly-fraction, cost-aware & G3: 50\% trade reduction \\
4 & Validating & Walk-forward OOS & G4: Sharpe $\geq 0.5$, PF $\geq 1.2$ \\
5 & Regime & GMM conditioning & G5: $1.5\times$ IC ratio improvement \\
6 & Multi-Freq & Timeframe ensemble & G6: composite $>$ individual \\
7 & Portfolio & Risk parity, cross-asset & G7: DD $< 0.8\times$ worst single \\
8 & Paper & Live simulation & G8: Sharpe $> 0.5\times$ backtest \\
9 & Deploy & Model readiness & G9: all artifacts ready \\
\hline
\end{longtable}

\subsection{3-Feature Liquidity Signal}

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Inputs}: \texttt{raw\_spread\_bps}, \texttt{raw\_ask\_depth\_5}, \texttt{flow\_vwap\_deviation}
    \item \textbf{Method}: Walk-forward z-scores (train on 3 prior dates), composite $= \frac{1}{3}(z_{\text{spread}} + z_{\text{depth}} + z_{\text{vwap}})$
    \item \textbf{Entry}: Long when composite $\geq P_{80}$, short when $\leq P_{20}$
    \item \textbf{Exit}: Fixed 100-minute horizon (20 five-minute bars)
    \item \textbf{Performance}: Sharpe 5.6--11.8 (symbol-dependent), net 6.7--7.2 bps/trade
\end{itemize}

\section{Layer 4: Execution}

\subsection{Paper Trading}

Two modes: \textbf{batch} (replay all dates, compare to backtest) and \textbf{watch} (daemon monitoring for new data). Gate G8 requires paper Sharpe $> 0.5\times$ backtest, IC decay $< 50\%$, max daily loss $< 2\%$.

\subsection{Signal Bridge}

Live execution daemon with 5-minute cycles:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Reads latest bars $\to$ computes signal $\to$ reconciles position
    \item Modes: dry-run, paper, live (requires \texttt{HL\_PRIVATE\_KEY})
    \item Kill switches: daily loss $> 1\%$, weekly DD $> 2\%$, monthly DD $> 5\%$, IC negative 5 days
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Capital Scaling}

Progressive deployment: 1\% $\to$ 5\% $\to$ 10\% $\to$ 25\% of equity. Portfolio-level drawdown control scales positions by 50\% if DD exceeds 2\%.
